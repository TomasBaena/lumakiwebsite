About Us

Lumaki was founded in 2015 in Waterloo Ontario Canada. The name "Lumaki" comes from the Filipino language Tagalog and means "To Grow". The company launched early November with the overall goal to grow our clients ideas by providing affordable services in website design, mobile app development and graphic design with a heavy focus on one-on-one consultations. Lumaki is a relatively small company so it only accepts a select number of projects at a time. This allows them to offer our clients a unique tailored experience making sure all their needs are met. Website Design

Websites connect you to your customers, clients and the world. Having a well designed functional website is a key factor to your business's success and overall image. We offer quality, from top-to-bottom services to get your website off the ground. We firmly believe all websites should have a mobile version that is tailored to the device in terms of style and functionality. We utilize modern technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JQuery, Bootstrap and Parse. We can also make you a custom Wordpress theme for when you want to be able to upload posts.

Mobile App Design

Have the next big app idea? Need a app to pair with your other products? Let's build it together! We develop web based hybrid apps for iOs and android operating systems. Some of the frameworks and tools we use are Ionic, Parse, AngularJS.

Graphic Design

Well designed graphics offer so much more than words. They have the ability to inform, excite, or teach the viewer in little time. Let us design a picture for your product, project, business, or hobby that is sure to get your point across. Working closely with you we are able to get not only the content but also the tone of your message. We use industry standard tools such as the Adobe Suite and Wacom graphics tablets.
